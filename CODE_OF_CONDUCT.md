# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to foster an open and welcoming environment.

## Our Standard
Contributions must be respectful, inclusive, and professional.

## Enforcement Responsibility
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing the code of conduct.

## Scope
Applies to all interactions in the repository, issues, and forums.

## Enforcement
Incidents will be reviewed and acted upon by the enforcement team.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Violations may result in warnings, suspension, or permanent ban.
